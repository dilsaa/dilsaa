About Me

SELENE elene SEZER class="font-['Times_New_Roman',serif]">ezer

I am a writer drawn to the cannot-be-defined territories of unsettling feelings that often elude language, shaping them into stories and occasional poems guided by metaphors born of nature. Inspired by antiquity, specifically its philosophies, and the architecture of language itself, I believe in simplicity when naming liminal emotions, and again, I believe that true introspection is clearest when unadorned. Thus, my work moves between intimate, introspective prose and a gentle magical realism touched by the echoes of old fairy tales. After earning my PhD in Behavioral Genetics and Evolution in 2023, I have continued to observe the hidden patterns of human nature and capture them relentlessly in my writing. I currently live in Oslo, Norway, am a proud board member of the Oslo Writers League, and am working on my second draft of my longform project alongside flash stories and a childrenâ€™s picture book project. Most importantly, I stand as a fierce advocate for both human and animal rights.
