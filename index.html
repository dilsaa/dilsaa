About Me

Selene Sezer

I write at the intersection of instinct and intellect. Inspired by the philosophies of antiquity and the stark beauty of the natural world, I seek to map the unsettling emotions we struggle to name. My stories are built on the belief that the deepest truths require the simplest language. Following a PhD in Behavioral Genetics and Evolution, I continue to observe the quiet rhythms of our species, capturing them in a blend of introspective prose and magical realism. Based in Oslo and active within its vibrant writing community as a board member of the Oslo Writers League, I am currently refining my second longform project, drafting flash stories, and collaboratively working on a childrenâ€™s picture book. Above all, my pen stands as a fierce advocate for both human and animal rights.
